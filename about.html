Our Story

A hidden gem. A coffee cart. A sanctuary.

Tucked Away &
Mellow Vibes.

We are an unassuming spot in Adams Normandie that you might almost miss—but the sign out front guides you in.

Espresso Social Club started as a concept: a coffee cart tucked inside a cozy space that feels both stylish and welcoming. We aren't a factory; we are a lounge. With artwork on the walls and couches to sink into, we curated a space for you to escape the noise.

As one of our regulars put it: "Simplicity but quality. That's how I'd describe the ambiance, decor, and drinks."

Meet The Team.

Coffee is chemistry, but a coffee shop is community.

When you walk in, you aren't just a number. You’ll likely be greeted by Joy, whose knowledge of coffee is matched only by her ability to make your day better with a conversation. Or you'll meet Gabby, known for her sweet demeanor and friendly service.

We believe in supporting small business values: paying attention to every detail, from the temperature of the oat milk to the art on your latte.

"Talk coffee, talk art, relax and don't think about the world a bit."
